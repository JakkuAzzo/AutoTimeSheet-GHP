About & Guidance

This tool runs entirely in your browser. To automate checks, upload the original Word .docx weekly timesheet using the GMT template. PDFs and scans are not supported for automatic parsing.

Tips for the Word file:

  • Keep the header row: DATE | WORKSITE ADDRESS | START | FINISH | LUNCH | BASIC HRS | O/T 1.5 | O/T 2.0.
  • Avoid merging columns or changing the order of columns.
  • Include week text like Week Number: 1 or Week Beginning: 27th October 2025 if possible.

All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
