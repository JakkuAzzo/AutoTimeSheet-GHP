3. About & Guidance

All processing happens locally. Enter data manually or upload a Word .docx timesheet. When ready, submit via the Submit Timesheet button. The submission email includes your name, date range and the CSV of entries.

  • Header row must remain standard.
  • Include week info text where possible.
  • Ensure Start/Finish use 24h HH:MM format.
  • Lunch: minutes or HH:MM.

All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
