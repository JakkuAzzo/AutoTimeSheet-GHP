3. About & Guidance

All processing happens locally, with auto-save to your browser. Hours (Basic, OT 1.5, OT 2.0) are automatically calculated from Start, Finish, and Lunch times. Review calculations in Step 2 before submitting.

  • Ensure Start/Finish use 24h HH:MM format.
  • Lunch: minutes or HH:MM.
  • Overnight shifts supported (finish time next day).
  • 40h basic hours per week required before overtime counts.
  • Data auto-saves as you type.

All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
